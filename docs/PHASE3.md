# Phase 3 Deferrals

Phase 2 deliberately does not claim native Android/iOS clients, subscriptions, supplier or enterprise marketplaces, automated identity verification, AI diagnosis/pricing, voice calls, phone masking, route optimization, professional fleets, recurring maintenance, loyalty/referrals/promotions, cross-border payments, multi-country tax, insurance automation or legal arbitration.

The web application remains mobile-first and every marketplace contract is under `/api/v1`, so native clients can reuse authentication, money, status, idempotency, event and permission semantics without moving business rules into an app UI.
